 Polar Platformer

 I created this small Polar Bear platformer in 2017 for my Intro to Programming class. It was made using Gamemaker 1, and I drew the bear and ice sprites myself. It was the first real game I ever made.

 Bear Pong

This was a silly little game we made to learn gamemaker in the beginning of the intro to programming class. It is a two player game, with one paddle controlled by "w" and "s", and the other controlled by the arrow keys.

 Fighting Fire with Fire!

This was the final game project we made for Intro to Programming last year. It involved platforming sections and a pokemon-style, randomly generated boss battle. All sprites were drawn by me."
